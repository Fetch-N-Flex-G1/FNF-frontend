Privacy Policy

1. Information We Collect

The Fetch N Flex App may collect the following information:

  • Basic pet profile information, such as name, breed, age, and weight.
  • Location data to track your pet's activity and exercise routes, if you grant us access to your device's location services.
  • Health and fitness data, including activity levels, exercise routines, and health goals you set for your pet.
  • Information you voluntarily provide, such as photos and notes about your pet's activities and health.

2. How We Use Your Pet's Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Track and analyze your pet's activity levels and provide insights into their fitness and health.
  • Personalize exercise and nutrition recommendations for your pet based on their profile and goals.
  • Improve our services and develop new features for the Fetch N Flex App.
  • Communicate with you about app updates, important information, and promotions.

3. Information Sharing

We do not share your pet's information with third parties without your explicit consent, except:

  • When required by law or in response to a legal request.
  • To service providers who assist us in delivering the app's features (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics).
  • If the app offers social features, such as sharing pet achievements, we may share limited information with your consent.

4. Data Security

We take the security of your pet's information seriously and have implemented measures to protect it from unauthorized access or disclosure.

5. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Please review this policy periodically for any updates.

6. Access and Control of Your Pet's Data

You may access and update your pet's information within the Fetch N Flex App. If you have questions or requests regarding your pet's data, you can contact our support team.
